Some common Roll reciepe

Ingedients


For the dough:

  • 3/4 cup of warm milk
  • 2 1/4 teaspoons yeast
  • 1/4 cup of granulated sugar
  • 1 egg + 1 egg yolk
  • 1/4 cup butter
  • 3 cups bread floor

FOr the filling:

  • 2/3 cup dark brown sugar
  • 1 1/2 tablespoons ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 cup butter
